The Tragedy of Time

By Dhwani Tekchandani


*A tale that every school-going kid deserves to tell*

The dreaded tricking began sooner than expected. She tried to plop open her eyes, but sudden bright light blinded her vision. She could faintly hear it, and then, there it was. Deafening. Her hands had violent motives, but the tears in her eyes pleaded for just five minutes more.

Thus, she snoozed her alarm.
