An Ode to My Grandfather

By Tripti Sharma

She got to know about the news as it spread
And cried and cried till her eyes were all red
She was left in complete shock,
For she never knew that he would no more talk.
His problems why could no one cure?
Two months of pain he tolerated what for?
When he was not to meet her back
And all colours for her changed forever to black.

Even though he lay on that bed for long ,
It always felt he was the same sturdy and strong
Who knew he was close to the end?
And that she was about to lose her intimate friend?
But she said he knew,
Two years earlier his end , he could view
All the pain , he silently hid, with that smile and charm,
His face reflected that he was at peace and forever calm.
The house was as silent as it could have ever been .
She mourned and wept at places where she couldn’t be seen.
She cried and cried till her eyes were deep red.
when she closed her eyes ,all she could see was him lying on that bed.

You see how her smile fades away..
And she seems to have lost all ways..
Oh almighty her strength you have to increase!
May his soul rest in peace
But only if all of this is true,
because the reality is that all her life is now blue.

The bond was not that of a grandfather and his granddaughter,
But simply of a father and his daughter.
She resided in his heart as he said,
held her hand tight even when on bed.
Oh supreme being think of that girl and the bond that they shared!
For him, she really cared,
and so did he.. See that girl, that girl is me.

He was a fighter for sure
,but his problems why couldn’t he cure??
Will she still have to move on in life?
With her heart broken into pieces ,as if cut with a knife.
Pieces so small, impossible to be put back to place,
Can all of this not be seen by looking at her face?
Purest of the pure souls he was..
For her, he was just perfect ,without any flaws.
And they say , what he deserved , he did attain.
As he entered a world where there was no loss and no gain.

She still feels he is here and hasn’t gone away,
but then why does he not make her feel his presence in any way??
She feels he is reading and so she writes..
And in his memory daily recites.
For him to listen and come to her soon,
for she feels alone and deprived of that cocoon
When they clap for her in praise,
she closes her eyes and wishes if she could go back to those days,
when she ran to him and shared all that happened during the day.
And he listened with full energy and zeal to what she had to say.
But now, he wont listen to her voice and his energetic face , she will not be able to see
It is not the same and will never be…
